Frequently Asked Questions

What types of denim does FashionID manufacture?

FashionID manufactures raw selvedge denim, stretch denim, organic cotton denim, overdyed denim, and recycled fiber denim. We support weights from 6oz lightweight chambray to 16oz heavyweight raw denim, with over 40 wash and finish options including stone wash, enzyme wash, acid wash, and laser distressing.

What is the minimum order quantity for custom denim production?

Our standard MOQ for denim production is 3,000 pieces per style per colorway. For sample development and new brand partnerships, we offer a reduced MOQ of 500-1,000 pieces at adjusted unit pricing. High-volume orders of 20,000+ pieces receive preferential FOB pricing.

How long does a denim production run take?

A standard denim production cycle takes 90-120 days from approved tech pack to ex-factory. This includes fabric sourcing (15-20 days), sample development (10-15 days), bulk production (45-60 days), and finishing/QC/packing (10-15 days).

Does FashionID offer sustainable or organic denim options?

Yes. We partner with OEKO-TEX certified mills to source BCI cotton, GOTS-certified organic cotton, and recycled fiber blends. Our dye houses use EIM-scored laser finishing to reduce water consumption by up to 70% compared to traditional stone washing.
