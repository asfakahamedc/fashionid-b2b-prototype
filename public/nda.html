Non-Disclosure & IP Protection Framework.

Last Updated: March, 2026

At FashionID Company, we recognize that your designs, patterns, and technical specifications are the lifeblood of your brand. Our operations are governed by this strict Non-Disclosure and Intellectual Property Protection framework to guarantee total operational integrity.

1. Definition of Confidential Information

For the purposes of our B2B engagement, "Confidential Information" encompasses any pre-release or proprietary brand assets submitted by you, including but not limited to:

  • Technical Packs (Tech Packs) and Digital CADs.
  • Bills of Materials (BOM), custom fabric compositions, and bespoke wash recipes.
  • Sizing specifications and computerized grading rules.
  • Physical prototypes, pre-production samples, and hardware moldings.
  • Brand artwork, heat-seal transfers, woven labels, and retail packaging layouts.

2. Strict Non-Disclosure Protocol

FashionID operates as a closed-loop sourcing proxy. We categorically will not share, distribute, or auction your Confidential Information to open markets or unauthorized third parties.

Your tech packs are exclusively routed to the designated production managers, pattern masters, and audited factory floor leads strictly assigned to execute the preliminary quoting, sampling, or bulk production run.

3. Sub-Contractor Binding (Crucial)

As an overarching manufacturing agency, it is our legal responsibility to enforce IP compliance downstream. FashionID enforces this identical NDA framework onto all partner cut-and-sew factories, dye houses, print facilities, and fabric mills within our approved matrix.

Under no circumstances are these partnered facilities permitted to produce unauthorized overruns of your garments, sell B-grade/rejected stock to local markets, or utilize your block patterns for other clients.

4. Non-Circumvention

FashionID guarantees that we will act solely as your manufacturing partner and proxy. We will not bypass, circumvent, or attempt to sell your developed apparel directly to your distributors, wholesalers, or retail customers. We do not operate competing generic apparel brands using your proprietary fits.

5. Return or Destruction of Materials

If, after the quoting phase, you do not proceed with bulk sampling or production through FashionID, you retain the immediate right to request a full purge of your assets.

Upon receipt of a written request, FashionID will permanently delete all digital files, Tech Packs, and email correspondences from our servers and ensure all physical PP prototypes or fabric swatches sent by you are either securely destroyed or returned at your shipping expense.

6. Formal NDA Execution

Before submitting sensitive Tech Packs or establishing a manufacturing relationship, many brands require a countersigned document. A formal, legally binding PDF version of this Non-Disclosure Agreement is available for corporate signature upon request.
