Mohammad Arif Akram

Managing Director

Arif Akram has spent 15+ years building deep operational expertise across Bangladesh's Ready-Made Garment (RMG) sector. From factory floor management to international trade negotiations, he oversees every aspect of FashionID's manufacturing pipeline.

His core specializations include denim production management, multi-factory sourcing coordination, international buyer relationship management, and strategic pricing architecture. Arif personally oversees the onboarding process for every new brand partner to ensure alignment on quality benchmarks, production timelines, and compliance requirements.

Under his leadership, FashionID has scaled from servicing regional buyers to managing production runs for brands across Europe, North America, and the Middle East.

Mohammad Adnan Akram

Director of Operations

Adnan Akram is the operational backbone of FashionID. His domain is the factory floor — from production scheduling and workforce allocation to real-time QC enforcement and AQL 2.5 compliance.

He specializes in production line optimization, multi-order scheduling across concurrent factory partners, pre-shipment inspection protocols, and material wastage reduction. Adnan ensures that every bulk order passes through a rigorous 4-point inspection framework before packing.

His hands-on approach to production management has consistently reduced defect rates and improved on-time delivery metrics to above 95% across FashionID's active factory partners.
