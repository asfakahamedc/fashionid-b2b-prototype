Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: March, 2026

FashionID Company ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and intellectual property of the global apparel brands we partner with. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and B2B lead generation services.

1. Information We Collect

As a B2B manufacturing proxy, we collect information specifically to facilitate the production lifecycle. The data we collect includes:

  • B2B Lead Capture Data: Company Names, Work Emails, Estimated Order Quantities (MOQs), and specific product category interests submitted via our inquiries forms.
  • Technical Specifications: Tech packs, design files, CADs, and manufacturing notes required for timeline estimation and accurate quoting.
  • Standard Analytics: Log files, IP addresses, browser types, and timestamp data collected automatically to ensure website functionality and security.

2. Protection of Intellectual Property

This is crucial to our operations.

We recognize that your designs, tech packs, and brand assets are highly sensitive. Any files submitted for quoting or sampling are held under strict confidentiality. These assets are never shared with unauthorized third-party factories, open markets, or competitors. Physical and digital access to your IP is restricted exclusively to our core merchandising team and the specific, verified production floor handling your account.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for B2B fulfillment:

  • Generating accurate production timeline estimations and indicative pricing.
  • Direct, secure communication regarding your order statuses and quality control updates.
  • Managing your account across our vertically integrated supply chain.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your corporate data. Data is only shared securely with verified logistics, shipping, and supply chain partners (e.g., freight forwarders, customs brokers) strictly when necessary to clear and deliver your finished garments.

5. GDPR & International Compliance

In accordance with international data regulations (including GDPR), you retain full rights over your corporate data. You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold, modify any inaccuracies, or request the complete deletion of your records and tech packs from our servers. To exercise these rights, please contact our compliance officer at the email listed below.
