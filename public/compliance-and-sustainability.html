Water Reduction (70%)

Laser distressing and ozone finishing replace traditional stone-wash processes, cutting water consumption by up to 70% per garment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What compliance certifications do your partner factories hold?

Our audited factory matrix holds BSCI, SEDEX/SMETA 4-pillar, OEKO-TEX Standard 100, WRAP, and ISO 9001:2015 certifications. Individual factory audit reports are available upon request under NDA.

How does FashionID ensure ethical labor practices?

We exclusively partner with factories complying with the Bangladesh Labour Act 2006 (amended 2018). All facilities enforce a maximum 60-hour work week, pay at least the RMG minimum wage, prohibit child labor, and maintain independent worker welfare committees.

Can I request a third-party factory audit before placing an order?

Absolutely. We can arrange buyer-nominated third-party audits through SGS, Bureau Veritas, or Intertek prior to order placement. Existing audit reports are also shared under NDA for qualified buyers.

Does FashionID support sustainable manufacturing?

Yes. We partner with ETP-certified dye houses, source BCI and GOTS organic cotton, use laser finishing to reduce water consumption by 70%, and support recycled polyester and Tencel™ lyocell blends with full material traceability.
