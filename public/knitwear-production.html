Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for knitwear?

Our standard MOQ is 1,000 pieces per style per color. For startup brands and sample runs, we accommodate reduced MOQs of 300-500 pieces. Pricing is volume-dependent, with significant per-unit reductions at 5,000+ and 10,000+ piece thresholds.

What knit fabrics does FashionID source?

We source single jersey, double jersey, interlock, pique, French terry, fleece, rib knit, waffle knit, and ponte roma. Compositions include 100% combed cotton, cotton-polyester blends, tri-blends, organic cotton (GOTS), and recycled polyester. GSM ranges from 120 to 400.

How long does knitwear production take?

Standard production takes 60-90 days from approved sample to ex-factory. Knitting and dyeing accounts for 20-30 days, cutting and sewing 25-40 days, and finishing/QC/packing 10-15 days. Lead times are shorter than woven garments due to the integrated pipeline.

Can FashionID do custom printing and embroidery on knitwear?

Yes. We offer screen printing (up to 12 colors), DTG (direct-to-garment) digital printing, sublimation, heat transfer, puff print, discharge print, and machine embroidery. All printing is done in-house to maintain quality control throughout the decoration process.
